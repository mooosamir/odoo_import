Product Bundle Pack on Sales

Product Bundle Pack Configurations

Under Sales > Configuration > Settings menu user have to select invoice and stock management options for pack items.

f user selects "Only main Bundle product Pack" as invoice option then only product bundle pack will available on invoice lines, and user selects "Deduct Stock Of Only Bundle Items" option as stock management then only product pack items quantity will deducted from stock.
